iii. The Director

A life devoted
to music.

Ariadna Benitez Talavera is a Cuban pianist and conductor with a distinguished international career. She began her piano studies at the age of four, and today combines decades of artistic experience with a profound pedagogical vocation.

She has served as Music Director of the Matanzas Symphony Orchestra in Cuba, leading national tours, premieres, and major festivals. She is currently pursuing her Doctor of Musical Arts in Orchestral Conducting at Boston University, and has guided young musicians toward conservatories and high-level programs across the Americas.
